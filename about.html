Get to Know Us

Pioneering Hands-On Hospitality Training

Twist Academy was established in Nepalgunj to empower local students with international-standard culinary, barista, and bartending capabilities.

Bridging the Gap Between Education and Careers

At Twist Academy, we observed that the traditional education path in Mid-Western Nepal left a huge gap in practical, career-ready skills. Hospitality establishments were constantly looking for skilled workers, while eager youths lacked specific training.

We created high-end simulation labs. Our student baristas practice on commercial double-boiler espresso machines, our bartenders practice with real bar layouts, and our bakery students work in a high-capacity oven deck.

The Labs

State-Of-The-Art Gastronomy Arenas

The Espresso Lab

Fitted with professional Italian grinders, multiple espresso machines, and a milk steam station for realistic cafe training.

The Bartender Arena

Features split-level cocktail wells, ice chests, speed rails, and bottle mockups for flair spin training and mixology sessions.

The Bakery & Kitchen

Equipped with commercial planetary mixers, temperature-controlled deck ovens, fermentation proofer boxes, and granite countertops.
