Our Curriculum

Professional Certification Courses

Designed to meet international hospitality standards, our training courses focus entirely on actual skill building, coffee recipe crafting, pastry preparation, and mixology structures.

Specialized Academy

Barista & Brew School

Level: Beginner to Pro

Our Barista training takes you through the entire bean-to-cup journey. Students practice dial-in calibrations on modern high-end grinders to adjust grind profile sizes, pull espresso at exact extraction ratios, and texture micro-foam milk for premium design latte art (hearts, rosettas, tulips).

What you will learn:

  • Espresso calibration, dosing, and tamp methods.
  • Milk texturing chemistry (micro-foam vs. dry foam).
  • Pouring techniques for basic and advanced latte art.
  • Manual filter methods (V60, Aeropress, Chemex).
  • Cafe machine cleaning, safety, and inventory setup.

Mixology Academy

Bar Academy (Flair & Bartending)

Level: Professional Certification

Master the physics and theater of professional beverage service. This curriculum focuses on classic mixology blueprints (sours, fizzes, highballs), flavor-balancing mocktail creations, fast multi-order cocktail build setups, and flair showmanship (working flair with bottles, tins, and shaker catches).

What you will learn:

  • Bar layout, tools, speed pouring, and measures.
  • Cocktail category blueprints and balancing math.
  • Working flair and exhibition flair techniques.
  • Molecular mixology (dry ice, smoke bubbles, foams).
  • Licensing, alcohol guidelines, and customer relations.

Gastronomy Program

Bakery & Pastry Arts Class

Level: Foundation to Intermediate

Delve into the chemistry of baking. Students work with planetary mixers and deck ovens to master yeast fermentation, dough laminations for croissants and danishes, baking sponge cakes, preparing chocolate glazes, and practicing piping designs for decorative icing cakes.

What you will learn:

  • Yeast microbiology and bread fermentation science.
  • Lamination logic for croissants and puff pastries.
  • Sponge cake baking, leveling, and assembly.
  • Fondant work, royal icing, and buttercream piping.
  • Commercial kitchen scaling, ovens, and sanitation.

Specialty masterclass

Specialty Sushi Class

Level: Masterclass

A focused culinary course dedicated to the traditional Japanese art of sushi making. Master the cooking and vinegar-seasoning profile of sushi rice, practice precise knife angles for slicing fresh seafood, roll Hosomaki, Futomaki, and inside-out Uramaki, and style elegant plating structures.

What you will learn:

  • Rice selection, rinsing, cooking, and hangiri seasoning.
  • Sushi rolling mats (makisu) control and seaweed (nori) prep.
  • Rolling Uramaki (California) and Futomaki profiles.
  • Sashimi and Nigiri slicing methods.
  • Plating presentations, soy, ginger, and wasabi balance.

Culinary Masterclass

Pizza & Burger Class

Level: Intermediate Masterclass

Learn the principles of Italian flour-water fermentation. Students explore dough hydration formulas, Neapolitan dough stretching (slap and stretch), woodfired temperature management, creating robust marinara sauces, and rolling out artisan fresh pasta sheets.

What you will learn:

  • Flour profiling (Tipo 00 vs Semolina) and hydration math.
  • Hand-stretching pizza dough (no rolling pins).
  • Woodfire oven fire management (400°C+ baking).
  • San Marzano tomato sauce recipes and cheese pairings.
  • Artisan pasta making (Fettuccine, Ravioli) from scratch.
